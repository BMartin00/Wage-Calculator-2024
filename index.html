How to Use the Wage Calculator

Enter Your annual gross salary and select your status from the drop-down box. The standard tax credit will be applied according to your status.

When you are done, click the "Calculate" button, and you will be able to see the gross income, total payable tax (PAYE), tax credit, net tax, PRSI, USC, total deductions, and the take home salary on annual, monthly, weekly, and daily bases in the table on the right.

The data shown is based on 2024 rates.


2024 RATES

Tax Rates (PAYE)

Single: €0 - €42000 @ 20%; over €42000 @ 40%

One Parent Family: €0 - €46000 @ 20%; over €46000 @ 40%

Married One Income: €0 - €51000 @ 20%; over €51000 @ 40%

Married Two Income: €0 - €84000 @ 20%; over €84000 @ 40%


Tax Credits

Single: €1875

One Parent Family: €5500 (PAYE: €1875 + Single: €1875 + Single Person Child Carer: €1750)

Married One Income: €3750

Married Two Income: €3750


PRSI Rates

Weekly income is less than 352.01: €0

Weekly income is 352.01: @4.1% - €12 PRSI Credit

Weekly income is between 352.01 and 424: is reduced by one sixth and taking €12 Tax Credit from it. Take this from the income @4.1%

Weekly income is over 424.01: @4.1%


USC Rates

€0 - €12012: @0.5%

€12012.01 - €25760: @2%

€25760.01 - €70044: @4%

over €70044: @8%
